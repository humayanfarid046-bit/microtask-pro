Privacy Policy - MicroTask Pro

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

MicroTask Pro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application, "MicroTask Pro".

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection

To provide our reward-based micro-task services, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your Name and Email Address. This is necessary for account identification, communication, and processing your earnings securely.
  • Device Information: We collect your Device ID, IP Address, and Android Advertising ID (AD_ID). These identifiers are used for fraud prevention, ensuring fair play, and delivering personalized advertisements.
  • Task Proof Data: When you complete a task, we collect the information you submit as proof (such as your username or order ID within the third-party platforms) to verify your earnings manually.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Tracking Earnings: To accurately track your completed tasks and coins.
  • Payment Processing: To ensure your earned money in INR reaches the correct user.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent multiple account creation on the same device and other fraudulent activities.
  • Customer Support: To assist you with any inquiries or issues related to your account or earnings.

3. Third-Party Services and Promotional Content

We partner with trusted third-party services to keep our app functional and provide task opportunities:

  • Database: We use InstantDB to store and manage your data securely.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may access your Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google’s own privacy policy for how they handle data.
  • Promotional Activities: We promote third-party Android applications. When you choose to install these applications via the Play Store, we do not interfere with the installation process; it is handled entirely by the Google Play Store.
  • Survey Services: We integrate links to external survey websites. Please note that when you navigate to these websites, their respective privacy policies will apply. We are not responsible for the data collected by those third-party sites.
  • Analytics: We use internal tracking tools to monitor app performance and improve user experience.

4. Data Security and User Rights

We value your trust and are committed to data security:

  • No Data Sale: We do not sell your personal data to any third-party companies.
  • Transparency: We clearly disclose that our app involves promotional content for third-party apps and external survey sites. Users are under no obligation to complete these tasks.
  • Data Deletion: You have full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so by using the "Delete Account" option available inside the app. Once you request deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our records.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon being posted on this page.
